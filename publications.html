Distributed Operating Systems Research (1994-1996)

Contributed to groundbreaking research on distributed operating systems based on the Mach microkernel at the MASI Laboratory, Paris 6 University (Sorbonne). This work formed the foundation for modern distributed computing concepts.

📅 1994-1996
🏛️ Paris 6 University

Research Focus:

  • Distributed system architecture based on Mach microkernel
  • Inter-process communication mechanisms
  • Resource management in distributed environments
  • Fault tolerance and system reliability
  • Performance optimization for distributed computing
