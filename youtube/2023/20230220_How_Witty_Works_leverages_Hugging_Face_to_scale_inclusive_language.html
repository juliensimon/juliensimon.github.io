February 20, 2023
During this webinar, Elena Nazarenko, Lead Data Scientist at Witty Works, Lukas Kahwe Smith, CTO & Co-Founder at Witty Works and Julien Simon, Chief Evangelist at Hugging Face, discuss how Witty Works leverages Hugging Face to scale inclusive language.
