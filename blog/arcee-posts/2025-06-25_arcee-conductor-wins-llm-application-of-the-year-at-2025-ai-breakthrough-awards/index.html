If you've ever used a Large Language Model (LLM) like GPT-4 or Claude 3.7, you know the magic—and the frustration. Sometimes, you’re left wondering: “ Is this the best model for this task? Am I overpaying for something a smaller model could handle just as well? ” We’ve all felt that FOMO—the fear that there’s a better, cheaper, faster model out there.

Building a Smarter Way to Work with AI

That’s exactly why we built Arcee Conductor . It’s not just a tool—it’s your AI control tower. Conductor intelligently routes each prompt to the best-fit model based on complexity, domain, and cost. Whether it's one of our Small Language Models (SLMs), such as Blitz, Virtuoso, or our newly launched Arcee Foundation Model, or a top-tier LLM, Conductor ensures you get the right model for each prompt, every time.

As our CEO, Mark McQuade, puts it: “ Today’s AI is about more than a single model. New AI models are released daily, and it’s our pioneering work in SLMs that gives us unique insight into which model is the right one for certain tasks or queries. ”

Whether you’re working with general-purpose queries, function calling, or reasoning, Conductor allows you to maximize cost efficiency without compromising performance.

Real Results, Real Savings

Users of Arcee Conductor have reported cost reductions of 65% or more, and up to 90% compared to relying exclusively on high-cost large language models (LLMs). By intelligently routing prompts to lower-cost models when appropriate, companies avoid unnecessary expenses while maintaining performance.
