About

Julien Simon is a leading figure in the AI and cloud computing landscape, renowned for his ability to demystify complex technologies and make them accessible to developers and enterprises alike. With over 30 years of experience, including leadership roles at Viadeo, Aldebaran, Criteo, and Pixmania, he has a deep understanding of scaling infrastructure and fostering innovation. His tenure at Amazon Web Services as a Principal Technical Evangelist for AI & ML further solidified his reputation, where he delivered hundreds of talks globally.

Currently serving as the Chief Evangelist at Arcee.ai, Julien is at the forefront of advocating for Small Language Models (SLMs) as a practical and efficient alternative to large-scale models. He emphasizes the advantages of running SLMs, highlighting their cost-effectiveness and suitability for various applications. His approach challenges the prevailing notion that larger models are inherently better, proposing instead that smaller, specialized models can deliver superior performance in many real-world scenarios.

Julien's philosophy centers on empowering organizations to take control of their AI solutions. He advocates for open-source tools and frameworks that enable enterprises to develop, fine-tune, and deploy models tailored to their specific needs. This approach not only enhances privacy and compliance but also fosters innovation and reduces reliance on proprietary systems.

Through his work, Julien continues to inspire and lead the AI community, demonstrating that with the right tools and mindset, organizations can harness the power of AI to drive meaningful change and achieve their objectives.
